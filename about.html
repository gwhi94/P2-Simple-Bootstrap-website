About the Festival!

Information on the Festival and Venue

Hello and welcome to the website of this years Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival. My name is Jennifer Viala and i am the organizer of the festival and founder of Brooklyn Vibes Events Co.

This year i shall be showing some of the best films to hit the stage in the last few years. Showings will happen from August 5th to the 8th at the wonderful Brooklyn Bridge Park, this excellent venue will allow me to show the films to an audience of 5,000 people!


My aim is to showcase a selection of films that will appeal to all ages and tastes, so expect plenty of films for all ages and a relaxed atmosphere at the Brooklyn Bridge Park.

Shows will be free and open to everyone! however due to the capacity limit i ask you to pre register if you wish to attend, be sure to do this as soon as possible as spaces are limited to 5,000.

Food Vendors at the Festival

At the festival, you are guaranteed a fun filled day. The festival and film line up will appeal to all audiences and ages, films are shortlisted and picked based on thir content and entertainment value. Refreshments and drinks will be on offer throughout the festival, from hotdogs, pizza, and hundreds of veggie options! see below for a list of refreshment vendors at the festival.

Dont forget you can also bring your own food and refreshments along, be sure to check out the Films page to see what will be shown this year.

About the Venue!

The Brooklyn Bridge Park is a world class 85-acre park on the Brooklyn side of the East River in New York city, the park itself is situated on a 2.1km stretch of Brooklyns post industrial waterfront, which has been renivated and revitalised. The park is open year round and often hosts events such as our Film Festival.


Getting to the park is easiest via public transport as parking is very limited. The best way of getting there is by walking, biking or by taking the bus, subway and ferry. See the map below for the location of the park.




The exact address for the park is 334 Furman St, Brooklyn Heights, NY 11201 United States

There is plenty more information regarding accessibility, rules and FAQ's over on the Brooklyn Bridge Park Website so go check it out!
