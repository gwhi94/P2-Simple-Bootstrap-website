Zoe Keepers Wife Added to Film Line-up

We have just added the Zoe Keepers Wife to the film line up this year. This film was decided on after several of us watched it fell in love with it. It tells the story of a woman who saved hundreds of people and animals during the German invasion of Warsaw in WW2.

Entry added 14/03/18 12.45

Brooklyn's Hotdogs and Burgers providing food for the festival

We have just confirmed that the renoun caterer, Brooklyns Hotdogs and Burgers will be catering and offering food and refreshments for the Festival. BH&b is one of the oldest independant caterers in New York, BH&b provide excellent quality food to all major events in New York and Brooklyn, a family run business, started in 1950. It is known for its great food at great prices.

Entry added 05/02/18 14.32

Personal Shopper Added to Film Line-up

We have just confirmed that the film 'Personal Shopper' will be shown at this years fetival! Kristen Stewart stars in this 2016 film as a personal shopper refusing to leave Paris until shes made contact with her twin brother, this mystery drama is an exciting watch throughout.

Entry added 03/02/18 09.45
